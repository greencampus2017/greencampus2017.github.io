Green Campus

Your Waste Is Our Resource

Waste production and its proper dispose is a big challenge.India produces 150 MMT of waste daily and Campuses like Universities, corporates,Government offices produces a lot of waste and it finally ends up in dumping sites due to mismanagement. Here comes an opportunity for us, waste for someone is someone else's resource. Green Campus works upon efficient waste Management system which further incorporates upcycling, recycling with skilled community involvement to generate useful products as well accelerate the process of building sustainable campus around. The involvement of people related with organisation makes it a much more responsible and eco friendly place to work. Lets practice rather than preach to make Earth as well as our campuses a better place to live.
